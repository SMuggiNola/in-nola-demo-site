About Irish Network NOLA

Learn what drives our organization, who we serve, and how we build community in New Orleans.

Mission Statement

Irish Network NOLA strengthens connections between Ireland, New Orleans, and the broader Gulf South by fostering cultural exchange, supporting community partnerships, and creating opportunities for collaboration, celebration, and shared heritage.

Who We Are

We are a diverse community of professionals, artists, students, families, and cultural advocates united by a shared connection to Ireland and a deep love for New Orleans.

What We Do
  • Host social, cultural, and networking events
  • Support Irish and Irish-American organizations
  • Build partnerships across business, culture, and education
  • Welcome newcomers and connect local communities
Our Values
  • Hospitality — Everyone belongs.
  • Community — We build together.
  • Heritage — We honor the past and carry it forward.
  • Connection — Local roots, global friendships.
Our Legacy

From early waves of Irish immigration to today’s vibrant cultural, professional, and civic contributions, Irish Network NOLA stands on generations of connection between New Orleans and Ireland. Our work continues a story rooted in resilience, hospitality, and shared identity.
