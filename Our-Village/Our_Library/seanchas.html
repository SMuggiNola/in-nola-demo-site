An Seanchas

The lore we hold together — the stories of our people, kept and passed on. Every family, every scéal, adds to the whole.

an seanchas — the lore entire, our whole holding of stories.
scéal — one story; scéalta — the stories.
So a family's telling is its own seanchas — seanchas mhuintir Mhongabháin, the seanchas of the Muggivan people — and all of them together make this collection.
