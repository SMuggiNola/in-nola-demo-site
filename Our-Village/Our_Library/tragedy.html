A Tragedy Waiting To Happen

By Tony and JJ Muggivan

A Tragedy Waiting To Happen is a profound exploration of family history, resilience, and the Irish experience, authored by Tony and JJ Muggivan.

A Note of Appreciation

We wish to express our sincerest thanks and warm appreciation to Tony and JJ Muggivan for so graciously allowing us to publish their book in its entirety here for all visitors of our website to experience.

Their willingness to share this powerful, challenging, and deeply personal narrative is a remarkable gift to our community. We are incredibly proud to feature "A Tragedy Waiting To Happen" as the inaugural contribution to The Stacks, marking the beginning of our mission to preserve and share the curated works of the Irish Community in New Orleans.
